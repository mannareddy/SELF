*SELF

“Who am I?”

…

Not something to be told.



This is not meant to be read fully.

Just scroll.

Stop where something clicks.



At some point,

you started looking for something.

and even when you found it…

it didn’t stay.



maybe it was success

or money

or meaning

or just… peace



want

get

lose

repeat



but what if it’s not supposed to be this way?



something is always there

aware of it all



*SELF

not defined

not given

not explained



or maybe this is nothing

you decide
