*SELF

“Who am I?”

…

Not something to be told.



This is not meant to be read fully.

Just scroll.

Stop where something clicks.




At some point,

you started looking for something.

and even when you found it…

it didn’t stay.



maybe it was success

or money

or meaning

or just… peace



something that felt like

“this is it”



but it never really stayed




so you moved on

to the next thing

and the next



without really stopping



to ask—

what am I actually looking for?




want

get

lose

repeat



and you’ve probably accepted this as normal


but what if it’s not?




what if this loop

is not how it’s supposed to be


but just something

you never questioned




because everything you experience

comes

and goes



every feeling

every moment

every achievement



it doesn’t stay




so what exactly are you expecting

to stay?



pause here for a second




because if everything changes


something must be there

that doesn’t



something that sees it all



thoughts come

and go


emotions come

and go



but something is always there

aware of it




you’ve noticed it before


just not clearly



that quiet sense of

“I am”




not something you created

not something you learned


just… there




but most of the time

it gets covered


by what you’re thinking

what you’re chasing

what you’re trying to become




so maybe this was never about

finding something new


but noticing

what was always there



not outside


but here




some call it awareness

some call it consciousness

some call it SELF



names don’t matter


because it’s not something

you can define


only something

you can see




this doesn’t mean

you stop living


you can still act

build

create


but without losing yourself in it




so again


who are you

before all of this?




You don’t need to understand all of this.

Just notice what makes sense.



If you prefer to explore further:


→ see everything at once

→ follow it step by step




“Who am I?”


not something to answer

just don’t ignore it



*SELF


not defined

not given

not explained



something you realize


or maybe this is nothing

you decide
