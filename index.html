*SELF

“Who am I?”

…

Not something to be told.



This is not meant to be read fully.

Just scroll.

Stop where something clicks.




At some point,

you started looking for

something.

and even when you found it…

it didn’t stay.



maybe it was success

or money

or meaning

or just… peace



something that felt like

“this is it”



but it never really stayed

did it?




so you moved on

to the next thing

and the next

and the next



without really stopping



to ask—

what am I actually looking for?




because if you look closely,

it’s always the same pattern



want

get

lose

repeat



and you’ve probably accepted

this as normal


but what if it’s not?




what if this loop

is not how it’s supposed to be


but just something

you never questioned




and after a while,

it starts to feel like


no matter what you chase

it ends the same way


so maybe—



the problem is not

what you’re chasing


but where you’re looking from



because everything you experience

comes

and goes



every feeling

every moment

every achievement



it doesn’t stay



so what exactly are you expecting

to stay?



pause here for a second




because if everything you experience

comes and goes


then something must be there

that doesn’t



something that sees it all



thoughts come

and go


emotions come

and go



situations change

constantly


but something is always there

aware of it




you’ve noticed it before


just not clearly



that quiet sense of

“I am”




before the thoughts

before the labels

before everything else


just… there




If you prefer to explore further:

→ see the whole picture

→ go deeper

“Who am I?”


not something to answer

just don’t ignore it



*SELF


not defined

not given

not explained



something you realize


or maybe this is nothing

you decide
