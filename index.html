*SELF

“Who am I?”

…

Not something to be told.



This is not meant to be read fully.

Just scroll.

Stop where something clicks.




At some point,

you started looking for

something.

and even when you found it…

it didn’t stay.



maybe it was success

or money

or meaning

or just… peace



something that felt like

“this is it”



but it never really stayed

did it?




so you moved on

to the next thing

and the next

and the next



without really stopping



to ask—

what am I actually looking for?




because if you look closely,

it’s always the same pattern



want

get

lose

repeat



and you’ve probably accepted

this as normal


but what if it’s not?




what if this loop

is not how it’s supposed to be


but just something

you never questioned




and after a while,

it starts to feel like


no matter what you chase

it ends the same way


so maybe—



the problem is not

what you’re chasing


but where you’re looking from



because everything you experience

comes

and goes



every feeling

every moment

every achievement



it doesn’t stay



so what exactly are you expecting

to stay?



pause here for a second




because if everything you experience

comes and goes


then something must be there

that doesn’t



something that sees it all



thoughts come

and go


emotions come

and go



situations change

constantly


but something is always there

aware of it




you’ve noticed it before


just not clearly



that quiet sense of

“I am”




before the thoughts

before the labels

before everything else


not something you created

not something you learned


just… there




but most of the time

it gets covered


by what you’re thinking

what you’re chasing


what you’re trying to become



so you start identifying with

everything that changes


and forget what doesn’t



and that’s where the confusion begins




so maybe this was never about

finding something new


but noticing

what was always there



not outside


but here




some call it awareness

some call it consciousness

some call it SELF



names don’t matter


because it’s not something

you can define


only something

you can see




this doesn’t mean

you stop living


or stop pursuing things


it just means


you stop expecting them

to complete you



you can still act

build

earn


create


but without losing yourself in it




that’s where

“*”

comes in



not fixed

not defined


open

like a wildcard


everything

and anything



42 is often called

“the answer”


but maybe it’s not an answer


maybe it’s pointing to something


to be

not become


just… be




when you see this clearly

something changes


not outside


but in how you move through it



less reaction

more clarity


less chasing

more direction



and from there

everything else

starts aligning




so again


the question is not

what should you do next


but


who are you

before all of this?




You don’t need to understand all of this.

Just notice what makes sense.



If you prefer to explore further:


→ see everything at once

→ follow it step by step




or you can ignore all of this

and continue as usual


nothing changes


until you look


not for an answer


but for what’s always been there


quietly




“Who am I?”


not something to answer

just don’t ignore it



*SELF


not defined

not given

not explained



something you realize


or maybe this is nothing

you decide
